FAQ
Common Questions
Is this tool completely free?

Yes! The core invoice builder — adding business details, items, auto GST calculation, preview, and PDF download — is 100% free. The free PDF includes a small watermark. Upgrade to Pro to get a clean watermark-free PDF, save invoices, and more.

When should I use IGST vs CGST/SGST?

Use CGST + SGST for intra-state supply (seller and buyer in same state). Use IGST for inter-state supply (seller and buyer in different states). InvoiceKaro auto-detects this.

Do I need GSTIN to use this?

You can still create invoices without a GSTIN — useful for businesses below the registration threshold (₹20–40 lakh). Simply leave the GSTIN field blank.

Is this valid for GSTR filing?

InvoiceKaro generates invoices in the format prescribed by GST rules. For GSTR filing, use the data from your invoices to fill GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, etc. via the GST portal.

Under India's Goods and Services Tax (GST) framework introduced in 2017, every registered dealer must issue a tax invoice for taxable supply of goods and services. A valid GST invoice must include the supplier's name and GSTIN, the invoice number and date, the buyer's details, HSN/SAC codes for each item, the applicable tax rate (0%, 5%, 12%, 18%, or 28%), and a clear breakup of CGST and SGST (for intra-state) or IGST (for inter-state transactions). InvoiceKaro generates invoices that include all of these mandatory fields automatically.
